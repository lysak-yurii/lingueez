# Delete your Lingueez account

This page explains how to delete your **Lingueez** account and the data linked to
it. It applies to the **Lingueez mobile app** (Android) and the **Lingueez
desktop app**, which share one account.

> ℹ️ A Ukrainian version is available [here](/legal/uk/delete-account/).

---

## Delete your account from within the app

**On Android (mobile):**

1. Open **Lingueez** and make sure you are signed in.
2. Tap the **Settings** (gear) icon at the top of the word list.
3. Tap **Delete account**.
4. Confirm when asked.

**On desktop:**

1. Open **Lingueez** and sign in.
2. Go to **Settings → Account**.
3. Choose **Delete account** and confirm.

Deleting your account is **immediate and permanent**.

## What is deleted

- Your **account** (email, sign-in details and profile).
- **All of your synced content** — every word, saved text and tag, and the
  timestamps and status/favourite flags attached to them.

This is removed **immediately and permanently** from our servers. There is **no
grace period and no way to recover it**, so please export or back up anything you
want to keep first (the desktop app offers an export).

## What is kept, and for how long

- **Nothing is retained after account deletion.** Once you delete your account,
  your account and synced content are gone for good.
- **Individual items you delete** (a single word, text or tag) — while your
  account still exists — are kept for a short grace period (up to 30 days) so the
  deletion can sync to your other devices, after which they are permanently
  removed.
- **Data stored only on your device** (app settings, offline cache and, on
  desktop, local backups) stays on that device until you remove it; it is cleared
  when you sign out.

## Can't open the app?

If you can no longer sign in, email us from the address on your account at
**[privacy@lingueez.app](mailto:privacy@lingueez.app)** and ask us to delete your
account. We will verify your request and remove your account and all associated
data, and respond within 30 days.
